M-Pesa Setup Required

Your M-Pesa payment credentials are not configured

1

Create Daraja Account

Visit Safaricom's Daraja Portal

Go to Daraja Portal

If you don't have an account, click "Sign Up" first

2

Create Sandbox Application

  1. Log in to Daraja Portal
  2. Click "Create App" button
  3. Fill in app details:
    • App Name: YarnQuest Payments
    • Description: M-Pesa payment processing for online yarn shop
  4. Click "Create"

You'll receive: Consumer Key and Consumer Secret

3

Enable Lipa Na M-Pesa Online

  1. In your app settings, find "Lipa Na M-Pesa Online"
  2. Click "Enable"
  3. You'll receive a Passkey - copy it!

You'll now have:
• Consumer Key
• Consumer Secret
• Passkey

4

Update Configuration File

Edit mpesa_config.py in your project folder:

MPESA_CONFIG = {
    'CONSUMER_KEY': 'your_consumer_key_here',
    'CONSUMER_SECRET': 'your_consumer_secret_here',
    'BUSINESS_SHORT_CODE': '174379',  # Keep for testing
    'PASSKEY': 'your_passkey_here',
    # ... rest of config
}

Replace the placeholder values with your actual credentials

5

Restart the Application

Stop and restart your Flask application:

Press CTRL+C in the terminal where the app is running

Then run: python app.py

6

Test Payment

Troubleshooting

❓ I can't find my Consumer Key/Secret
In Daraja Portal:
  1. Log in to your account
  2. Select your app
  3. Go to "API Keys" section
  4. You'll see Consumer Key and Consumer Secret there
❓ Where is the Passkey?
  1. In your app, find "Lipa Na M-Pesa Online"
  2. The Passkey is displayed when you enable this service
  3. Copy it and save it in mpesa_config.py
❓ Still getting "failed to access token"?
Check these:
  • All three credentials are correctly copied
  • No extra spaces before/after credentials
  • Application has been restarted after updating config
  • Check the terminal for error messages
❓ How do I get production credentials?
  1. In Daraja, upgrade your app to production
  2. Contact Safaricom support to activate production keys
  3. You'll get production Consumer Key/Secret
  4. Update BASE_URL to: https://api.safaricom.co.ke
  5. Update your config and restart
